<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Zenith Inc Prestige Mechanic">

In the world of this Roblox incremental simulator, **Zenith Inc prestige** acts as the primary engine for long-term growth. While the immediate effect of a prestige is the loss of your current coins and tower floor progress, the long-term benefits are essential for reaching the end-game content. By performing a prestige, you convert your temporary efforts into permanent power.

This system is designed to overcome the natural "walls" or plateaus encountered during tower climbing. As the cost of upgrades increases exponentially, your standard income will eventually fail to keep up. Resetting allows you to return to the start with significantly higher earning potential.

| Feature | Pre-Prestige State | Post-Prestige State |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coins** | High (Reset to zero) | Zero (Fast accumulation) |
| **Tower Floors** | Current highest floor | Floor 1 (Unlocked faster) |
| **Multipliers** | Base x1.0 | Permanent Boost (e.g., x2.5+) |
| **Prestige Points** | Zero | Earned based on progress |
| **Automation** | Basic | Enhanced via upgrades |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Note">
The amount of Prestige Points you receive is directly tied to your highest floor reached and total coins earned during that specific run. Pushing just five extra floors can sometimes double your point yield.
</Callout>

</Section>

| **Milestone Near** | **Push** | If a major milestone (e.g., Floor 250) is 2 floors away, finish it. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Premature Resets">
Performing a **Zenith Inc prestige** before reaching at least Floor 50 is generally inefficient unless you have specific quest requirements. The time taken to climb back usually outweighs the tiny bonus gained.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Prestige Points and Upgrade Priority">

Once you have successfully reset, you will have **Prestige Points** to spend in the specialized upgrade shop. These upgrades are permanent and do not disappear during future resets. Choosing the right order for these upgrades is critical for maintaining momentum.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Global Income Multipliers">
    Always invest your first batch of points into multipliers. This ensures that every coin you earn from Floor 1 is worth significantly more, making the early-game "slog" non-existent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automation Speed">
    Upgrade how fast your auto-clickers and auto-collectors function. In Zenith Inc, AFK (Away From Keyboard) efficiency is the key to overnight progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Floor Skip Chance">
    Later in the prestige tree, you can unlock the ability to skip floors entirely. This is the single most important stat for late-game farming runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Retention">
    Unlock the ability to keep a small percentage of your coins or specific upgrades after a reset. This drastically reduces the setup time for new runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade Name | Priority | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Zenith Multiplier** | **High** | Increases all coin gains by 50% per level |
| **Auto-Climb Bot** | **Medium** | Automatically advances floors when possible |
| **Gem Finder** | **Medium** | Increases chance of Gems appearing in rewards |
| **Prestige Boost** | **Low** | Increases points gained in the *next* prestige |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Tip">
Prioritize "Multipliers" over "Flat Additions." In an incremental game like Zenith Inc, multiplicative growth will always outperform additive growth within a few hours of gameplay.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Prestige Strategies: Rebirth and Ascension">

As you progress further into Zenith Inc, you will encounter even deeper reset mechanics beyond the standard prestige. These systems, often referred to as **Rebirth** or **Ascension**, require you to reach much higher floor milestones (such as Floor 1000) but offer rewards that change the fundamental rules of the game.

| Mechanic | Requirement | Major Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Prestige** | Floor 50+ | Prestige Points & Multipliers |
| **Rebirth** | 10k Prestige Points | New Upgrade Trees & Skill Points |
| **Ascension** | Floor 1000+ | Permanent Tower Perks & Rare Crystals |

These systems work in layers. You will prestige many times to afford a single Rebirth, and Rebirth many times to achieve Ascension. This "prestige-on-prestige" loop is what allows for the massive numerical growth characteristic of the genre.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Strategy">
When aiming for a Rebirth, stop spending Prestige Points on minor upgrades about 3 runs beforehand. Saving your points allows you to trigger the Rebirth mechanic faster and access the superior skill tree.
</Callout>

</Section>
