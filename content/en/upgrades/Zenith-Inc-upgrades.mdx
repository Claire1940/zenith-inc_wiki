<Section icon={Shield} title="Comprehensive Currency Allocation">

An efficient upgrade strategy requires a deep understanding of the game's multi-layered economy. Zenith Inc utilizes several distinct currencies, each designed to fund specific upgrade tracks. To prevent progression bottlenecks, you must treat each currency as a tool for a specific phase of your tower climb. Coins should be spent freely on immediate stat boosts, while Gems and Prestige Points must be hoarded and spent exclusively on permanent, high-impact multipliers that persist across resets.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Optimal Prestige and Rebirth Strategy">

The Prestige mechanic is the cornerstone of long-term progression in Zenith Inc. When you perform a Prestige, you reset your current Coins and basic upgrades in exchange for powerful Prestige Points. These points are used to purchase permanent upgrades that provide massive, global multipliers to your income and growth speed.

Timing your Prestige resets is crucial. Resetting too early yields too few points to make a noticeable difference, while waiting too long wastes valuable time that could have been spent climbing faster with new multipliers. The optimal strategy is to reset as soon as your progress on the current floor slows down to a crawl.

| Prestige Tier | Points Required | Upgrade Multiplier Boost | Recommended Focus |
| Tier 1 | 10 | 2x overall income | Early auto-income scaling |
| Tier 2 | 50 | 5x overall income | High-floor milestone pushes |
| Tier 3 | 250 | 15x overall income | Gem-based upgrade optimization |
| Tier 4 | 1200 | 50x overall income | Advanced tower floor climbing |

<Callout type="tip" title="Prestige Timing">
Before triggering a Prestige reset, check your potential Prestige Point yield. If completing one more floor takes more than five minutes, trigger the reset immediately. The multiplier boost from your new points will allow you to blast past your previous record in a fraction of the time.
</Callout>

</Section>
