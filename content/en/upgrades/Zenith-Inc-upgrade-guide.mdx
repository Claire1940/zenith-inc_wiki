<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Upgrade Path">

To maximize your efficiency, follow this optimized step-by-step upgrade path designed to take you from a fresh corporate recruit to a high-climbing executive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Basic Earning">
    Begin by focusing entirely on **Click Power**. Click actively to accumulate your first few thousand Coins. This initial pool of resources will fund your transition into automation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock and Maximize Auto Income">
    As soon as **Auto Income** becomes available, shift your investment away from active clicking. Upgrade Auto Income until it generates resources faster than your manual clicks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Multipliers for Exponential Scaling">
    Once your passive income is stable, start investing in **Multipliers**. These upgrades multiply your base Auto Income, turning small continuous gains into massive payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize AFK and Capacity Systems">
    Before logging off or leaving the game running in the background, invest in **Offline Earnings** and **Capacity**. This ensures your earnings do not cap out while you are away.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prepare for the Prestige Reset">
    When your progression slows down significantly and upgrading becomes too expensive, stop purchasing basic upgrades. Save your remaining resources and prepare to execute a **Prestige** reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid the Capacity Trap">
Do not over-invest in Capacity during the early game. If you are actively playing, your storage will rarely fill up, making early Capacity upgrades a waste of valuable Coins.
</Callout>

</Section>
