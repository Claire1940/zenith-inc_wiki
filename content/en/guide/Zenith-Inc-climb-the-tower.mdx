The transition from Rebirths to Ascensions marks the shift from mid-game to end-game. While Rebirths give you a percentage boost, Ascensions and the Ticket Shop introduce Stars, which act as a separate multiplier layer. This "multi-layered" scaling is what allows players to reach the astronomical numbers seen on the global leaderboards.

| Progression Tier | Currency Required | Typical Bonus |

<Section icon={Zap} title="Advanced Boosting: Tickets and Stars">

Once you have mastered the basics of how to **Zenith Inc climb the tower**, you will encounter the Ticket Shop and the Star system. Tickets are often earned through milestones or purchased via the shop. These tickets can be redeemed for Stars at specific locations like the Temple or the Tower Guardian.

The Star system is currently one of the most powerful mechanics in the game. Unlike standard multipliers, Stars seem to affect the core value of every step you take on the tower. This makes them significantly more valuable than standard Zenith Path boosts in the long run.
