<Section icon={Map} title="Starter Progression and Core Mechanics">

Navigating the early stages of Zenith Inc requires a balance between active clicking and strategic reinvestment. As a Roblox incremental simulator, the game rewards players who understand how to transition from manual labor to a fully automated corporate empire. Your primary objective is to climb the tower floors, each representing a new tier of income and difficulty.

In the first 10 floors, your focus should be entirely on **Click Power** and **Initial Income Boosts**. These upgrades are inexpensive and provide the necessary capital to unlock the first tier of automation. Without automation, your progress will stall during the mid-game transition.

| **Late Game** | Floors 501-1000 | Maximize AFK efficiency | Ascension Mechanics |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Zenith Inc Tips and Tricks for Prestige">

The Prestige system is the heart of Zenith Inc's progression. It requires you to reset your current Coins and Floor progress in exchange for **Prestige Points**. This mechanic is the only way to overcome the massive health and cost scaling found in the upper floors of the tower.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Slowdown">
    Monitor your floor climbing speed. If it takes more than 2 minutes to clear a single floor, you have reached your current "soft cap."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Current Run">
    Before hitting the Prestige button, spend all your remaining Coins on any upgrades that increase your "All-Time Earnings" stat, as this often influences the number of Prestige Points received.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Reset">
    Confirm the Prestige. You will be sent back to Floor 1, but with a significant global multiplier that will make the early floors trivial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reinvest Prestige Points">
    Immediately spend your points on **Global Multipliers** and **Automation Speed** in the Prestige Shop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Prestige">
Do not Prestige the moment it becomes available. Often, pushing for just 5 or 10 more floors can double the amount of Prestige Points you receive, making the subsequent run much more efficient.
</Callout>

As you progress further, you will unlock **Rebirth** and **Ascension**. These are "Super-Prestige" mechanics that reset even your Prestige upgrades in exchange for even more powerful, permanent buffs. These should only be performed when you have completely exhausted the potential of your current Prestige tier.

</Section>
