<Section icon={Shield} title="Prestige and Rebirth Mechanics">

The most complex part of **Zenith Inc how to play** is mastering the reset mechanics. Prestige is not a loss; it is a strategic reinvestment. When you Prestige, you lose your current Coins and Floor progress, but you gain Prestige Points (PP). These points are spent in a permanent shop that makes your next run significantly faster.

**Prestige Strategy Steps:**
1. **Push to the Limit**: Only Prestige when you can no longer afford any meaningful upgrades.
2. **Check the Yield**: Look at the "Points Gained on Reset" counter. If it's less than 10% of your current total PP, keep pushing.
3. **Spend Wisely**: Prioritize "Income Multiplier" and "Floor Unlock Discount" in the Prestige Shop.
4. **Speedrun Back**: Use your new bonuses to reach your previous highest floor in a fraction of the time.

| Reset Type | Requirement | Permanent Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Prestige** | Floor 100+ | Prestige Points for Multipliers |
| **Rebirth** | Floor 500+ | Rebirth Tokens for Tech Tree nodes |
| **Ascension** | Floor 2000+ | Massive global boosts and new Currencies |

<Callout type="info" title="The Prestige Rule">
The first Prestige is always the hardest. Once you have your first few upgrades in the Prestige Shop, the game transitions from a slow crawl to a high-speed simulator.
</Callout>
