<Section icon={Map} title="Zenith Inc Progression Guide: Understanding the Core Loop">

Zenith Inc, the premier Roblox incremental simulator developed by Peak Incrementals, challenges players to scale an infinitely growing corporate tower. The core gameplay loop revolves around generating basic resources, purchasing strategic upgrades, unlocking automation, and executing timely progress resets. By understanding how these systems interact, you can avoid progression bottlenecks and climb the corporate ladder at maximum speed.

To succeed in Zenith Inc, you must manage a multi-layered economy. Each currency serves a specific purpose in your growth pipeline, transitioning you from active clicking to completely automated, idle resource generation.

| Currency | Primary Source | Main Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Coins | Clicking, Auto Income | Basic upgrades, Click Power |
| Gems | Milestones, Rewards | Premium upgrades, Boosts |
| Prestige Points | Prestige resets | Permanent multiplier unlocks |
| Tokens | Events, Achievements | Limited upgrades, Bonuses |
| Crystals | Advanced systems | High-level unlocks |
| Cash | Gameplay activities | General upgrades |

<Callout type="tip" title="Multiplier Synergy">

</Callout>

The relationship between these currencies determines how quickly you can scale the tower. Coins allow you to purchase the raw upgrades needed to reach higher floors, while Gems and Prestige Points provide the exponential multipliers required to break through late-game progression walls.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Your First Upgrade">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Auto Income">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Climb Your First Tower Floors">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Complete Your First Prestige">

  </Step>
</Steps>

Active clicking becomes obsolete very quickly in Zenith Inc. Your primary goal in the first 15 minutes of gameplay should be establishing automated passive income.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Strategies: Prestige, Rebirth, and Ascension">

Late-game progression in Zenith Inc requires a deep understanding of reset mechanics. When your progress slows to a crawl, resetting your current tower progress is the only way to secure the massive multiplier boosts needed to climb higher.

There are three primary reset mechanics in the game, each operating at a different scale of progression.

| Reset Type | Cost/Requirement | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Prestige | High floor progress | Earns Prestige Points, permanent multipliers |
| Rebirth | Advanced milestones | Unlocks unique progression paths and systems |
| Ascension | Late-game limits | Extreme power scaling and exclusive bonuses |

<Callout type="tip" title="Optimal Reset Timing">
Never wait too long to reset. If climbing a single floor takes more than five minutes of active play, execute a Prestige reset immediately to acquire your multipliers.
</Callout>

By balancing these three reset systems, you can maintain a steady progression rate and avoid the stagnation that often affects unprepared players in the late-game stages.
