<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Rebirth and Scaling System">

The heart of Zenith Inc is its rebirth mechanic. Every time you rebirth, you lose your current Zenith and stair progress, but you gain a permanent multiplier that applies to all future earnings. This is a standard incremental trope, but in Zenith Inc, the scaling is particularly aggressive. You can go from earning thousands to earning quadrillions in a single play session if you manage your resets correctly.

As you progress, you will notice that the game uses a tiered naming system for large numbers (Million, Billion, Trillion, Quadrillion, Quintillion, Sextillion, etc.). Keeping track of these tiers is vital for knowing when you are ready for the next big upgrade.

| Currency Tier | Value (Scientific) | Typical Rebirth Bonus |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

To reach the leaderboard in Zenith Inc, you need a disciplined approach to your first hour of gameplay. Follow these steps to maximize your efficiency and avoid getting stuck on the lower tiers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Wealth Accumulation">
    Begin by climbing the stairs manually. Do not buy any low-level game passes yet. Your goal is to reach your first 100,000 Zenith. Use this to buy the basic "Zenith Amount" upgrades in the stats menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Rebirth Cycle">
    Once you hit 1 Million Zenith, perform your first Rebirth immediately. This grants a 15% bonus. Repeat this process until you have at least a 100% total bonus. This makes reaching the Billion tier trivial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilizing the Baths">
    Locate the **Zenith Bath** and **Sun Bath** on the map. These areas provide temporary but massive boosts to your income. Standing in the Sun Bath while your multipliers are active is the fastest way to jump from Billions to Trillions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Entering the Ticket Shop">
    Upon reaching the Trillion tier, you will unlock access to the **Ticket Shop**. Use your accumulated Zenith to buy tickets. These tickets can be spent at the Temple for "Stars," which provide a different layer of power.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Temple Guardians and Stars">
    Interact with the **Tower Guardian** at the Temple. Here, you can use your stars to get permanent buffs that do not reset upon rebirth. This is the "End Game" loop where you climb to reach the highest rankings on the leaderboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Secret">
You can actually rebirth *while* climbing. If you have enough Zenith for a rebirth, don't wait to fall off or reach a platform—trigger the rebirth from the menu to save precious seconds.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Mechanics: Tickets and Stars">

Once you move past the basic rebirth loop, Zenith Inc introduces the **Ticket and Star system**. This is where the game transitions from a simple clicker to a more complex management sim. Stars represent a secondary currency that doesn't scale as fast as Zenith but provides much more impactful long-term benefits.

| Item/System | How to Obtain | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tickets** | Purchase in Ticket Shop | Used for Star rolls |
| **Stars** | Spent at the Temple | Permanent stat multipliers |
| **Temple Surge** | Random Event | Temporary 10x multiplier |
| **Tower Guardian** | NPC Interaction | Unlocks Star upgrades |

Understanding the Ticket Shop is crucial for late-game players. Tickets increase in price exponentially, so you must balance your Zenith spending between "Immediate Multipliers" (Rebirths) and "Long-term Multipliers" (Tickets).

  <Card title="Star Focus">

  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info" title="Note on Stars">
Stars are currently one of the most mysterious mechanics. While they provide multipliers, the exact "Star Value" (e.g., 0.4 Stars) often relates to your standing with the Tower Guardian and your luck in the Ticket Shop rolls.
</Callout>
