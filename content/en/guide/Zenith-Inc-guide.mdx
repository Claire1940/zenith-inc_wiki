<Section icon={Target} title="Zenith Inc Beginner Roadmap">

Starting your journey in Zenith Inc can feel overwhelming due to the rapid scaling of costs. The core gameplay loop revolves around generating income, climbing floors, and resetting for better power. In the early stages, your focus should be entirely on establishing a consistent flow of Coins.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Collection">
    Begin by clicking actively to generate your first batch of Coins. While clicking is the slowest method, it is necessary to unlock your first few automated systems. Focus on reaching the first 10 floors as quickly as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Basic Automation">
    As soon as you have enough Coins, invest in **Auto Income**. This allows you to generate resources without constant manual input. Automation is the backbone of any incremental simulator and should be your primary investment target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Floor Milestone Pushing">
    Each floor increases the difficulty but also the rewards. Aim for **Floor 50** as your first major milestone. Reaching this point unlocks additional progression systems that make your first Prestige run significantly more efficient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The First Prestige Reset">
    Once your progress slows down and the cost of the next upgrade exceeds your current earning potential, it is time to Prestige. This will reset your Coins but grant you **Prestige Points**, which provide permanent growth bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip for Beginners">
Do not wait too long for your first Prestige. If it takes more than 10 minutes to reach the next floor, you are likely better off resetting and using the new multipliers to blast through the early stages again.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Currency Economy & Management">

Zenith Inc utilizes a multi-layered currency system to keep the progression feeling fresh and rewarding. Understanding when to hoard and when to spend each resource is critical for long-term success. The economy is divided into primary progression currencies and rare premium resources.

| Currency | Earning Method | Primary Usage | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coins** | Clicking, Auto-income, Floors | Basic upgrades, Click power | High (Early) |
| **Gems** | Milestones, Daily rewards | Premium boosts, Special unlocks | Medium |
| **Prestige Points** | Prestige resets | Permanent multipliers | Critical |
| **Tokens** | Events, Achievements | Limited-time rewards, Cosmetics | Low |
| **Crystals** | Late-game progression | Advanced systems, End-game power | High (Late) |
| **Cash** | Gameplay activities | General purchases, Utility items | Medium |

The interaction between these currencies creates the "incremental" feel. For instance, **Prestige Points** directly boost the rate at which you earn **Coins**, creating a feedback loop that allows you to reach higher tower floors in every subsequent run.

<Callout type="info" title="Economic Insight">
Always check your **Daily Rewards** and **Milestones**. These are the most consistent ways to earn **Gems** without spending real money. Gems are best used for permanent "2x" multipliers if they are available in the shop.
</Callout>

Advanced players often use a "Deep Run" strategy where they push as far as possible once a week, and "Speed Runs" during the rest of the time to farm Prestige Points quickly.

| Strategy | Goal | Active Time | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speed Run** | Rapid Prestige Points | 10-20 mins | Farming multipliers |
| **Deep Push** | New Floor Milestones | 2-4 hours | Unlocking mechanics |
| **AFK Farm** | Coin accumulation | 8+ hours | Long-term growth |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Note">
During a **Speed Run**, ignore expensive late-game upgrades. Focus only on the cheapest automation and multipliers to reach your target Prestige threshold as fast as possible.
</Callout>
