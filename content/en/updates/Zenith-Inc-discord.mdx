<Section icon={Target} title="Zenith Inc Beginner Progression Guide">

Starting your journey in Zenith Inc requires a shift in mindset from active clicking to strategic automation. The initial goal for any new player is to establish a self-sustaining economy. While clicking provides a temporary boost, the real power lies in the incremental systems that work while you are AFK.

Following a structured path ensures you do not waste resources on inefficient upgrades. The community frequently updates the **Zenith Inc beginner guide** on Discord to reflect balance changes in the early-game economy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Resource Collection">
    Focus on active gameplay for the first 10-15 minutes. Use your starting Coins to purchase basic Click Power and early Auto-Income nodes. This phase is about reaching the first 10 floors of the tower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automation Investment">
    As soon as you can afford it, shift all spending to Auto-Income. This allows your resources to grow without manual intervention. Check the **Zenith Inc discord** for the current "Golden Ratio" between Click Power and Automation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Tower Climbing">
    Push through the first 50 floors. Each floor increases your base multiplier. Do not worry about speed yet; focus on unlocking the first set of Milestone rewards which provide Gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="First Prestige Run">
    Once your progress slows significantly (usually around Floor 75-100), it is time to Prestige. This resets your Coins but grants Prestige Points, which are essential for long-term growth.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Early Stagnation">
Do not wait too long to perform your first Prestige. If it takes more than 5 minutes to unlock a single floor, you are likely better off resetting to gain the permanent multiplier bonuses.
</Callout>
