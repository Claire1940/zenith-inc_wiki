Avoid using **Gems** for "Instant Coin" packs. As you progress, the amount of Coins you get from these packs will be eclipsed by your natural growth within minutes. Always invest in **Multipliers** instead.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Tower Progression: Step-by-Step Guide to Floor 100">

Climbing the tower is the primary goal in Zenith Inc. Each floor requires a higher "Income Per Second" (IPS) to unlock. If you find yourself stuck on a specific floor, it usually means your **Zenith Inc best upgrades** are out of balance. Follow these steps to reach the first major milestone efficiently.

  <Step num="1" title="The Click Phase (Floor 1-20)">
    Focus entirely on **Click Power** and **Basic Multipliers**. Your goal is to generate enough Coins to unlock the first tier of **Auto Income**. Don't worry about AFK earnings yet; active play is much faster here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automation Setup (Floor 21-50)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Multiplier Wall (Floor 51-80)">

  <Step num="4" title="Prestige Preparation (Floor 81-100)">
    Focus on reaching Floor 100. This is the first major checkpoint where a **Prestige** becomes highly profitable. Ensure your **Offline Earnings** are upgraded so you continue to gain value if you take a break before the reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>
