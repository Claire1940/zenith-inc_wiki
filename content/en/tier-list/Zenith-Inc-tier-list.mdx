<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Climbing the tower in Zenith Inc requires a systematic approach. Many new players make the mistake of hoardings Coins or investing in low-tier upgrades too early. Following a structured progression path ensures that you hit your milestones without getting stuck in long periods of stagnation.

The journey from a trainee to a corporate titan involves mastering the reset mechanics. Each floor presents a higher difficulty curve, requiring more resources to unlock the next level. By following the steps below, you can optimize your path through the first 100 floors and beyond.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Basic Income">
    Begin by clicking actively to generate your first batch of Coins. Use these to purchase the first five levels of Click Power. This initial boost allows you to clear the first 10 floors in minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automate the Process">
    As soon as Auto Income becomes available, prioritize it over everything else. Your goal is to reach a point where the game plays itself while you focus on managing the larger economic strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Tower Milestones">
    Push toward Floor 50. Reaching this milestone unlocks the ability to earn Gems, a premium currency used for permanent upgrades that persist even after a reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Your First Prestige">
    When your progress slows to a crawl (usually around Floor 100), it is time to Prestige. This will reset your Coins and basic upgrades but grant you Prestige Points, which provide a massive, permanent multiplier to all future earnings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Prestige Timing">
Do not Prestige too early. Wait until the bonus you receive at least doubles your current earning potential. Resetting for a 10% boost is rarely worth the time it takes to rebuild your tower floors.
</Callout>

</Section>
