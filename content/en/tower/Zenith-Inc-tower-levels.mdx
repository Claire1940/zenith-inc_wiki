<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rebirth and Ascension Strategy">

To conquer the highest **Zenith Inc tower levels**, you must master the art of the reset. There are two primary forms of resetting progress: Rebirths and Ascensions. Rebirths provide a flat percentage boost to your Zenith income, while Ascensions usually unlock new mechanics, maps, or ticket-related bonuses.

Choosing when to rebirth is a balance between current speed and potential growth. If you are climbing stairs and your Zenith count is barely moving, it is time to rebirth. The game allows for "stacking" points, where you can wait to collect multiple rebirths at once, which is often more efficient than resetting every single time a rebirth becomes available.

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Temple and Star System">

The Temple is perhaps the most misunderstood mechanic in **Zenith Inc tower levels**. It acts as a secondary progression layer that doesn't reset upon rebirth. By using tickets—which can be purchased with gems or earned through specific milestones—players can gain Stars. These Stars are the key to unlocking the "Jungle Pull," a massive multiplier that is required to reach the Artto currency tier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Tickets">
    Earn tickets by reaching specific tower heights or purchasing them in the Ticket Shop using your accumulated Zenith or Gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Temple">
    Navigate to the Temple area, usually located near the Sun Bath. Look for the Tower Guardian NPC to initiate the ticket exchange.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll for Stars">
    Use your tickets to roll for Star multipliers. These provide a permanent "Four Times" (or higher) boost to your overall production.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Jungle Pull">
    Once you have enough Stars, you can activate the Jungle Pull mechanic, which provides a massive, non-diluting multiplier to your Zenith gains.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Star Mechanics">
Stars are not consumed like Zenith. They act as a permanent stat on your character profile, meaning every subsequent rebirth becomes easier the more Stars you have collected.
</Callout>
