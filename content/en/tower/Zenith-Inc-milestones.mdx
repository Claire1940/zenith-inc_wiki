<Section icon={Map} title="Core Progression and Economic Milestones">

In the world of Zenith Inc, progress is measured by your ability to scale a massive corporate tower through incremental upgrades and resource management. Understanding the various **Zenith Inc milestones** requires a deep dive into the game's multi-layered economy. Players start with basic manual clicking to generate Coins, but the true journey begins when you transition into automated systems.

The economy is built on five primary resources, each serving a specific role in your advancement. Managing these effectively is the difference between getting stuck at Floor 50 and reaching the end-game heights.

| Currency | Primary Earning Method | Critical Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coins** | Clicking / Auto-income | Basic upgrades, Click power, Capacity |
| **Gems** | Milestone Rewards / Tasks | Premium boosts, Exclusive progression items |
| **Prestige Points** | Performing a Prestige Reset | Permanent multipliers, Speed boosts |
| **Tokens** | Achievements / Events | Limited-time upgrades, Bonus rewards |
| **Crystals** | Advanced Progression | Ascension unlocks, High-level improvements |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always prioritize spending Coins on "Auto Income" upgrades before "Click Power." While clicking feels faster initially, the compounding nature of automation is what allows you to hit major tower milestones overnight.
</Callout>

As you accumulate these resources, your focus will shift from simple accumulation to optimization. The early milestones are designed to teach you the balance between reinvesting your earnings and saving for the next big floor unlock.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Upgrade Priority Tier List">

To reach your **Zenith Inc milestones** as quickly as possible, you must follow an efficient upgrade path. Not all upgrades are created equal; some provide immediate but short-lived boosts, while others offer long-term compounding value.

The following tier list ranks the available upgrade categories based on their impact on tower climbing speed and resource generation efficiency.

| Upgrade Type | Tier | Impact Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Auto Income** | **S** | Essential for AFK progress and consistent growth |
| **Multiplier** | **S** | Exponentially increases the value of all other upgrades |
| **Click Power** | **A** | Vital for early-game bursts and breaking through plateaus |
| **Offline Earnings** | **A** | Maximizes resource gain while the game is closed |
| **Capacity** | **B** | Increases storage limits, useful for long AFK periods |
| **Speed** | **C** | Provides minor efficiency gains in collection cycles |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Advice">
In the early game, aim for a 3:1 ratio of Auto Income spending to Click Power. Once you reach the mid-game (Floor 200+), pivot almost entirely to Multipliers and Prestige-based bonuses to maintain momentum.
</Callout>

Focusing on S-tier upgrades ensures that your resource generation keeps pace with the rising costs of higher floors. If you find yourself unable to afford the next floor within 10-15 minutes of active play, it is a sign that your Multipliers are under-leveled.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Mastering the Prestige System">

The Prestige system is the most critical of all **Zenith Inc milestones**. It involves resetting your current Coin-based progress and tower floor in exchange for Prestige Points. These points provide permanent, global multipliers that make every subsequent run significantly faster.

Knowing when to reset is an art form. Resetting too early yields too few points, while waiting too long wastes time on slow progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Floor 100">
    This is the minimum requirement to unlock the Prestige mechanic. Pushing slightly further to Floor 110 or 120 can often double your initial point gain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze Earning Speed">
    When your Coin generation slows down to the point where upgrades take several minutes to purchase, it is time to consider a reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Prestige">
    Trigger the reset. You will lose your Coins and current floor, but you will retain your Gems and gain Prestige Points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reinvest Prestige Points">
    Spend your new points on permanent Multipliers in the Prestige Shop. This will allow you to reach Floor 100 again in a fraction of the time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Strategy">
The "Optimal Reset Point" is generally when you can double your total lifetime Prestige Points. If you have 100 points, don't reset until the current run offers at least another 100.
</Callout>

</Section>
