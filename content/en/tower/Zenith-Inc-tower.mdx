<Section icon={Swords} title="Rebirth Mechanics and Zenith Inc tower Scaling">

To dominate the **Zenith Inc tower**, you must master the Rebirth system. Rebirthing resets your current Zenith and tower height but grants a permanent percentage-based multiplier to all future earnings. In the early game, reaching 1 million Zenith is your first major hurdle. Once achieved, a rebirth provides a 15% bonus. As you reach higher tiers like 1 billion, this bonus jumps to 30%, allowing you to breeze through the lower sections of the tower that previously took minutes to climb.

| Rebirth Tier | Zenith Required | Bonus Multiplier | Progression Impact |
|--------------|-----------------|------------------|--------------------|
| **Starter** | 1 Million | 15% | Faster early stairs |
| **Novice** | 1 Billion | 30% | Unlocks Billion-tier upgrades |
| **Intermediate** | 1 Trillion | 50% | Essential for Ticket Shop |
| **Advanced** | 1 Quadrillion | 100% | Rapid scaling to Sexilian |
| **Elite** | 1 Quintillion | 250% | Leaderboard contention |

The scaling in Zenith Inc is aggressive. By the time you reach the "Nano" or "Atto" tiers of currency, the multipliers from previous rebirths ensure that you are earning millions per second even at the base of the tower. This creates a "snowball effect" where each subsequent rebirth takes less time than the last, despite requiring significantly more currency.

<Callout type="warning" title="Scaling Warning">
The game scales "hard" once you hit the trillion mark. Ensure you have upgraded your ladder speed before attempting to push past the mid-tower sections, or you will find the climb tedious.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Early Game Progression Steps">

If you are new to the **Zenith Inc tower**, following a structured path is the fastest way to reach the leaderboards. The game provides a quest line that guides you through the initial mechanics, but the real strategy lies in optimizing your bath usage and ticket spending.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Ascent">
    Climb the stairs manually until you reach the first Zenith Bath. This will boost your amount significantly. Do not rebirth until you hit at least 1 million Zenith to ensure the 15% bonus is locked in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking the Sun Bath">
    Once you have rebirthed 2-3 times, push higher into the tower to find the Sun Bath. This area provides a much higher multiplier than the standard Zenith Bath and is the key to hitting the billion-tier milestones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ticket Shop Entrance">
    After reaching 1 billion Zenith, head to the Ticket Shop. Use your currency to buy tickets, which are then used at the star machine to gain permanent Star multipliers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascension and Glimmer">
    Continue the cycle until you reach Glimmer One. At this stage, your focus shifts from simple climbing to maximizing "Temple Surge" and "Tower Guardian" bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade Type | Priority | Effect | Best Use Case |
|--------------|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Ladder Speed** | High | Faster climbing | Active play sessions |
| **Zenith Multiplier** | Max | Increases all income | Always upgrade first |
| **Bath Efficiency** | Medium | Buffs temporary zones | Before a long push |
| **Star Chance** | Low | Better ticket rewards | Late game optimization |

<Callout type="success" title="Success Strategy">
Focus entirely on Zenith Multipliers for your first 5 rebirths. The faster you earn, the faster you can buy the more expensive speed upgrades later.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Systems: Tickets, Stars, and Temples">

Beyond the basic climb of the **Zenith Inc tower**, advanced players must interact with the Ticket and Star systems. Tickets are a secondary currency used to roll for Stars. Stars act as a global multiplier that does not reset upon rebirth, making them the most valuable long-term asset in the game.
