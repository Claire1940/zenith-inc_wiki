<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Zenith Inc Floors and Tower Mechanics">

Climbing the **Zenith Inc floors** is the heart of the experience in this Roblox incremental simulator. Unlike standard clicker games where numbers simply go up, Zenith Inc structures its growth around a vertical tower system. Each floor you reach acts as a testament to your resource generation efficiency and your strategic investment in upgrades. As you ascend, the difficulty curve steepens, requiring players to transition from manual clicking to sophisticated automated systems.

The tower is not just a visual representation of your score; it is a gated progression system. Each floor requires a specific amount of Coins or energy to "unlock" or pass. In the early stages (Floors 1-20), players can rely on active gameplay. However, as you approach the triple digits, the game shifts into a true incremental simulator where multipliers and Prestige bonuses become the only viable way to advance.

Zenith Inc utilizes a multi-layered economy. Understanding which currency to use for which upgrade is vital for climbing **Zenith Inc floors**. Coins are your "disposable" income, while Gems and Prestige Points are your "capital" investments.
