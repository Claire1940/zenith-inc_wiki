<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Your First Ascension">

Ascension is the ultimate goal for any new player in the **Zenith Inc roblox game**. It resets your Zenith and Rebirths but provides a permanent "Ascension Point" that can be used for the most powerful upgrades in the game. Follow these steps to reach your first Ascension efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Grind">
    Climb the stairs until you reach 1 Million Zenith. Do not buy every small upgrade; focus only on those that increase your Zenith per step. Perform your first Rebirth immediately upon hitting the requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Trillion Threshold">
    Repeat the climb, utilizing the 15% bonus. Aim for 1 Billion Zenith next to unlock the 30% bonus. Continue this loop until your total Zenith reaches the Trillions. This is where the game truly opens up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ticket Shop Optimization">
    Once in the Trillions, visit the Ticket Shop. Use your accumulated tickets to roll for Stars. Even a 4x Star multiplier can shave hours off your grind to the next tier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Map Transition">
    Reaching the required Ascension threshold will allow you to unlock the New Map. This map features better scaling buttons and more frequent Sun Bath opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Ascension Push">
    Stack your Jungle Pull and Star multipliers. Wait for a Temple Surge event to push your numbers into the Quadrillions and beyond, then trigger your first Ascension.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Ticket Usage">
Tickets are generated over time and through specific milestones. Never let your ticket count sit at the maximum capacity; always use them to keep your Star multiplier growing.
</Callout>
