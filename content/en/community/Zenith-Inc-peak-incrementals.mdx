<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Progression Strategy">

To optimize your journey, you must follow a structured progression path. This involves balancing active stair climbing with passive multiplier accumulation. Focus on short, fast rebirth cycles in the early game, transition to star farming in the mid-game, and finish with Temple upgrades to push your multipliers into the sexdecillions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Stair Climbing">
    Begin by climbing the stairs and stepping on every button. Purchase the Zenith Bath upgrade as soon as possible. Perform your first rebirth immediately upon reaching 1,000 points. Repeat this cycle 5-10 times to build a solid baseline multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sun Bath Transition">
    Once you reach Tier 2 Rebirth, pivot your focus to the Sun Bath upgrade. This upgrade scales multiplicatively and will allow you to reach millions of points in seconds. Continue rebirthing to maintain a high progression speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ticket and Star Farming">
    Upon reaching the billions, unlock the Ticket Shop. Spend your earned tickets to roll for stars. Equip your best stars to massively boost your overall multiplier. Do not ignore the lower-tier stars, as they still provide valuable cumulative bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Temple Optimization">
    In the late game, invest your excess points into the Temple upgrades. Focus on the Temple Surge first to increase your ticket acquisition rate, followed by the Tower Guardian to speed up your physical climbing on the stairs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Progression Phase | Target Multiplier | Key Upgrade | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 (Early)** | 1x - 10x | Zenith Bath | Rapid Rebirths |
| **Phase 2 (Mid)** | 10x - 1,000x | Sun Bath | Unlock Tickets |
| **Phase 3 (Late)** | 1,000x+ | Stars & Temple | Maximize Multipliers |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">

</Callout>
