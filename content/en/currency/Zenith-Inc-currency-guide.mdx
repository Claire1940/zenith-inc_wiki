<Section icon={Target} title="The Prestige Process: Resetting for Power">

The most critical part of any **Zenith Inc currency guide** is the Prestige system. Prestige allows you to trade your current Coins and floor progress for Prestige Points. These points provide a permanent multiplier to all future income, effectively "speeding up" time for your next run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the Prestige Threshold">
    Monitor your progress. Once your floor climbing speed slows down significantly and upgrades become prohibitively expensive, you have reached the "soft cap" for your current run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Calculate Potential Gains">
    Check the Prestige menu to see how many Prestige Points you will earn. Aim for at least a 2x-5x increase in your total multiplier before committing to a reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Reset">
    Trigger the Prestige. This will return you to Floor 1, but your new permanent multiplier will make the early stages take seconds instead of minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Re-invest Points">
    Spend your Prestige Points on the specialized Prestige Upgrade tree. Focus on "Income Multiplier" and "Upgrade Cost Reduction" first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Prestige Timing">
The best time to Prestige is when it takes more than 10 minutes to reach the next floor milestone. Efficiency is about "Points Per Hour," not the highest floor reached in a single sitting.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Callout type="info" title="Currency Synergies">
