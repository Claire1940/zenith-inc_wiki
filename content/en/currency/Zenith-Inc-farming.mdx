<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Farming Setup">

For new players, establishing a consistent farming rhythm is essential for reaching the mid-game content. Follow these steps to set up an efficient resource pipeline that supports both active and idle playstyles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Coin Accumulation">
    Begin by actively clicking and completing the first 10 floors. Use these initial Coins to unlock the first level of "Auto Income" as quickly as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automation Integration">
    Once Auto Income is active, reinvest all earnings into "Multiplier" upgrades. This creates a feedback loop where your automated income grows faster than your manual clicks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Milestone Pushing">
    Push toward Floor 50. Reaching this milestone unlocks additional reward systems and Gems, which should be saved for permanent multiplier boosts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prestige Preparation">
    When progress slows down (usually around Floor 100), stop buying minor upgrades. Save your Coins to push for the highest possible floor to maximize your Prestige Point gain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Prestige Reset">
    Trigger your first Prestige. Use the resulting Prestige Points to buy permanent upgrades that boost your Coin and Gem gain for all future runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Success">
By following this loop, players can typically reach Floor 500 within a fraction of the time it takes to play purely through manual clicking.
</Callout>

</Section>
