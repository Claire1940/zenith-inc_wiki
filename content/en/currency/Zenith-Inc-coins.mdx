<Section icon={Coins} title="Understanding Zenith Inc Coins and Economy">

In the world of Zenith Inc, coins represent the fundamental building block of your corporate tower empire. As an incremental simulator, the game revolves around generating these coins at an ever-accelerating rate. Unlike premium currencies, coins are earned through active clicking, automated systems, and reaching specific floor milestones within the tower.

To progress efficiently, you must understand where coins sit within the broader economic ecosystem of the game. While Gems and Prestige Points offer powerful late-game utility, coins are what keep your immediate upgrades flowing.

| Currency | Primary Source | Main Usage | Impact Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Farming Zenith Inc Coins">

Starting your journey in Zenith Inc requires a structured approach to ensure you don't hit a progression wall. By following these steps, you can optimize your coin flow from the very first floor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Manual Foundation">
    Begin by clicking actively to generate your first 1,000 coins. Immediately invest these into Click Power (Level 5) and your first Auto-Income generator. This creates a baseline of wealth that grows even when you pause.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Automation Shift">
    Once you reach Floor 10, shift your focus entirely to Auto-Income and Multipliers. At this stage, manual clicking starts to lose its effectiveness compared to the sheer volume of coins generated by automated systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Floor Milestone Pushing">
    Use your accumulated coins to unlock floors 1-50. Each floor increases the base coin reward for both clicking and automation. Do not stay on low floors for too long; the reward scaling on higher floors is significantly better.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The First Prestige">
    When coin progression slows down (usually around Floor 100), perform your first Prestige reset. You will lose your current coins and basic upgrades but gain Prestige Points that provide a permanent, massive multiplier to all future coin earnings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Milestone Reward">
Reaching Floor 100 for the first time unlocks the 'Corporate Executive' badge, which grants a permanent 1.2x multiplier to all coin farming activities in 2026.
</Callout>

</Section>
